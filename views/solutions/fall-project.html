Fall Prevention Project

Integrating Fall Risk Data into Workflows to Enhance Preventative Care

It is important to utilize data to identify people who are at risk for falls, and it is critically important to incorporate this information into the clinical workflow so that clinicians can be automatically alerted to the risk. The National Center for Collaborative Healthcare Innovation (NCCHI) looks to revolutionize the way VA Medical Centers approach and mitigate fall risk among Veterans within their patient population.


A diverse multi-disciplinary team of internal and external partners is working to integrate existing fall risk data into clinical workflows to improve the identification and visibility of Veterans at high risk for falls and ultimately improve their access to quality, tailored preventative care.

The innovation can also automate downstream workflows related to the fall risk, which ensures that all patients are efficiently and systematically identified to have the most appropriate and timely personalized care.
