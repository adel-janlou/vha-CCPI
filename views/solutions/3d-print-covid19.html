VHA 3D Printing Network COVID-19 Response

VA has entered into a memorandum of understanding (MOU) with the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) and National Institutes for Health (NIH) 3D Print Exchange to share data and coordinate on open-source medical products for the COVID-19 response. We are also working closely with America Makes to provide resources that will connect health care providers and 3D printing organizations. Finally, VHA facilities can submit a 3D printing need for consideration and tracking to the VHA Procurement & Logistics Office.
