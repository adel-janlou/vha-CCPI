Innovation Accelerator Program

Fundamentals

Innovation, while at times messy, has significant foundational structures. The following are the fundamentals of what we teach, preach, and practice:

The Three Box Solution

The strategies and mindsets needed to create the future are vastly different than, and potentially at odds with, those needed to respond to the present. The Three Box Solution teaches us how to balance it all to ensure that our organization doesn't become obsolete.

Human-Centerd Design (HCD)

Simply put, HCD helps us design solutions around the problems humans experience. Steeped in empathy, HCD is an iterative approach to problem-solving that involves true human perspective at every step of the way.

Lean Startup

Not to be confused with Lean Six Sigma, Lean Startup teaches us to favor customer feedback over lengthy planning processes, the art of prototyping, and when to pivot versus persevere- essentially anything related to finding out, at the very earliest opportunity, if an idea is on target or not.

Pitching and Storytelling

Two-hour presentations have their place in the world, but thankfully not within iNET. Learning into story evokes an emotional response, while a well-structured pitch can have leaders and investors seeing value within minutes.
