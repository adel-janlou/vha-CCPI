Fellowships

The VHA Innovation Ecosystem (VHA IE) Innovation Fellowship Program is a unique learning opportunity for emerging leaders with a dynamic presence, passion for innovation, and vision for the future.

Our Fellowship Opportunities

The Entrepreneur in Residence (EIR) fellowship provides an opportunity for emerging leaders who are prepared to implement innovation projects that leverage internal stakeholders and leaders at their local institutions and beyond.



Meet our Entrepreneurs in Residence »

The Senior Innovation Fellowship (SIF) provides an opportunity for accomplished leaders who are prepared to implement a national scale project that leverages internal and external stakeholders throughout the organization, government, academia, and industry with mature, large-scale innovation projects that are ready to further deploy.

Meet our Senior Innovation Fellows »

The MIT Catalyst Fellowship Program aims to spark innovative and impactful solutions to address unmet medical and health care needs in VA. Catalyst Fellows work to generate solutions using a needs-driven approach alongside a collaborative, multidisciplinary, nationally networked community. Projects with demonstrated potential for impact will establish longer-term efforts to realize impactful solutions.

Meet our Catalyst Fellows »

More about the Innovation Fellowship Program

The VHA IE Fellowship program focuses on developing the thought leaders of tomorrow to enable innovations that transform VA health care. During the year, Fellows are able to uncover insights, collaborate to build networks, and foster change through idea sharing. By offering emerging innovation leaders the opportunity to gain hands-on experience building solutions at scale, this program is an incredible opportunity for VA innovators to develop professionally and have a lasting impact on Veterans’ lives.
