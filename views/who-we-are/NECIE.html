VA New England Center for Innovation Excellence (NECIE)

The VA New England Center for Innovation Excellence (NECIE) provides the Innovation community with practical healthcare provider input to the development process, a natural clinical partner, and real-world patient input from the Veteran community as part of the design to accelerate “innovation to impact”. NECIE and the VHA Innovation Ecosystem are exceptionally aligned and positioned to design, develop, and broadly share positively impactful health care solutions.

Mission - Focus

The mission of VA New England Center for Innovation Excellence is to create the foundation for operationalizing innovation in VISN 1 and VHA’s health care settings.



The focus of VA New England Center for Innovation Excellence is three-fold

1. Technology solutions for chronic and/or complex illnesses and injuries
2. Rural access to world class health care
3. Health care and life-style solutions for aging veterans
