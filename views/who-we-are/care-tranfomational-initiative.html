Care & Transformational Initiatives

Testing New Models of Care for Transformational Impact

Care & Transformational Initiatives (CTI) is focused on testing and refining innovative care models and transformational initiatives that can be meaningfully scaled to impact Veteran care. CTI works closely with a variety of partners from academia, industry, non-profits, and other government agencies.
