Our Model

VHA Shark Tank Competition

VHA IE identifies employee-developed promising practices and matches them with interested facilities.


Facilitated Replication

Promising Practices area implemented in one or two additional facilities with training and project management support through a six to nine-month process.

Diffusion Pathway

Promising Practices continue through organic diffusion, initial diffusion, or national diffusion.

The Diffusion Marketplace

Diffusion Marketplace supports VA in becoming a high-reliability and learning organization by diffusing promising practices. The site is designed to help organically spread important and life-saving practices throughout the VA health care system.

Please note, at this time, the site is only accessible to VHA staff.
