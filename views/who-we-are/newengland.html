The mission of VA New England Center for Innovation Excellence is to create the foundation for operationalizing innovation in VISN 1’s health care settings.

V i s i o n

The vision of VA New England Center for Innovation Excellence is to enhance VISN 1's ability, through Innovation, to be the premier, integrated, academic health care system in New England, with a highly engaged team, providing exceptional, highly reliable care to the Veterans we serve.

Health care and life-style solutions for aging veterans

The VA New England Center for Innovation Excellence was created to serve as the premier, integrated academic health care system. The Center will focus on technology solutions for chronic illness, making high quality health care accessible to rural Veterans and providing health care solutions for an aging population.
