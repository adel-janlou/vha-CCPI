Learning Management (LM)

SimLEARN’s Learning Management Portfolio provides management, leadership, and educational expertise related to short- and long-range simulation-based training solutions to be delivered to VA Health Care Centers, field consultation, field-based intake with the eventual goal of managing enterprise simulation curriculum. 


Mission

Facilitate simulation-based development, curricula, distribution, and tools that supports enterprise level innovative healthcare solutions.

Vision

Be the premier resource to the enterprise for the development and implementation of simulation-based education.

Values

Functions

Advance standard of simulation education and learning by identifying best practices and innovative learning strategies.
