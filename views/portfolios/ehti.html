Emerging Healthcare Technology Integration (EHTI)

SimLEARN’s Emerging Health Technology Integration (EHTI) Portfolio provides unique value as VHA’s portfolio with the primary aim to integrate emerging technologies into clinical workflow through its use of simulation to (1) inform user training and (2) analyze and redesign workflow for optimized care delivery.


Mission

Accelerate the integration of emerging health technology that optimize patient care and empower VHA as a learning health system.

Vision

Care for Veterans is continuously enhanced through the integration of emerging health technology into clinical practice.

Values

Functions


Assess the technology landscape and identify solutions that can advance the standard of clinical practice


Inform SimLEARN with the integration of emerging health technologies into continued professional development opportunities for VHA health providers to advance patient care


Optimize workflows inclusive of emerging health technologies in a simulated, risk-free, environment
