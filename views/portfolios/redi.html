Resuscitation Education and Innovation (REdI)

The Resuscitation Education and Innovation (REdI) program, within SimLEARN, in its commitment to VA employees to provide the right tools, processes, and technologies to respond during a medical emergency. REdI oversees a robust resuscitation training portfolio that includes an innovative learning platform and a REdI Mock Code Training Program (RMCTP) that provides local VA Medical Facilities a standardized, turn key solution to building a mock code training program. In addition, REdI provides services through Resuscitation Simulation System Testing, which is a simulation-based strategy to identify and mitigate potential hazards related to the medical emergency response services and training of code team staff and first responders.
